ANCHOR TAG , PARAGRAPH TAG , PRE DEFINED OR PRE FORMATED TAG

ANCHOR TAG


Anchor tag is used to display the videos of other websites like youtube by using their links.

examples for anchor tag :


anchor tag test 1
anchor tag test 2

PARAGRAPH TAG

P tag is short for paragraph tag .P tag is used to write the content in paragraphs.


example for paragraph tag :


A farmer had a goose that laid one golden egg a day. He would sell the golden eggs, and they enjoyed a comfortable life. However, the farmer became greedy and wanted more than one egg a day. His wife foolishly agreed to his idea. The next day the farmer cut open the goose after it laid the golden egg. He could only find blood and guts. He realised his mistake. He now had no source of income, and the couple became poorer every day.



PRE DEFINED TAG


 PRE tag is used to write the content in pre defined formats


example for pre defined tag :


   There are simple steps and order in which you make tea :

   Fill up the kettle with water
   Boil the kettle
   Place a teabag in your favourite mug
   Pour boiling water into your favourite mug
   Brew the tea for a few moments
   Remove and dispose of the teabag
   Add milk
   Add sugar
   Stir the tea
   Enjoy the hot beverage
